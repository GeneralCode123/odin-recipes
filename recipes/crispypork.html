Crispy Pork

Crispy Pork features tender, flavorful pork belly with irresistibly crunchy skin. After boiling to achieve juicy meat, the pork skin is dried and deep-fried until golden and crackly, creating a perfect balance of soft and crispy textures that pairs wonderfully with dipping sauces or as a main dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly, skin on
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • Water (enough to cover pork during boiling)
  • Cooking oil (for deep frying)

Steps

  1. Boil pork belly in water with garlic, salt, pepper, and vinegar until tender (about 45 minutes).
  2. Remove pork, pat dry, and let it cool completely.
  3. Score or prick the pork skin to help it crisp.
  4. Rub a little salt on the skin and refrigerate uncovered for a few hours or overnight to dry out.
  5. Heat oil and deep-fry pork belly until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Slice into bite-sized pieces and serve with dipping sauce.
